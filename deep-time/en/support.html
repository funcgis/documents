About the app

Deep Time is an immersive deep-work space. Use the radial hub to choose mode and atmosphere, run a Deep Session, follow progress on themed Live Activities / Dynamic Island, and review finished sessions in Deep Log.

FAQ

  • How do I start a session?
    Tap the large time display to start or pause. Long-press to edit the duration for the current mode.
  • How does the radial hub work?
    Tap the center dot to expand, then choose Mode, Theme, Color, or Sound. Tap empty space to collapse.
  • Why is there no Live Activity / Dynamic Island?
    You need a compatible device and Live Activities enabled in system Settings. A themed Live Activity appears after you start a Deep Session.
  • No ambient sound?
    Check the Ring/Silent switch and system volume. Start a session or tap play. Some themes auto-match their ambient audio.
  • Where is Deep Log?
    Use the Deep Log control under the timer. Entries are added when a session completes.
  • Will I lose my data?
    Preferences and Deep Log are stored on device. Uninstalling the app removes local data.
